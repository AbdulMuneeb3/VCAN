Key Features

Connecting Volunteers and NGOs

VCAN provides a seamless platform for volunteers to discover meaningful opportunities and for NGOs to manage their volunteer programs.

  • Easy Volunteer Matching

    VCAN's intelligent matching algorithm connects volunteers with opportunities that align with their skills and interests.

  • NGO Management Tools

    NGOs can easily manage their volunteer programs, track hours, and communicate with their volunteers.

  • Impact Tracking

    VCAN provides powerful analytics and reporting tools to help NGOs measure the impact of their volunteer programs.
